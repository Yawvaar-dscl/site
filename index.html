About Delta Strategies

At Delta Strategies, we are dedicated to providing unparalleled risk analytics, treasury, margin, cash and financing consultancy and advisory services.

We partner with institutional investors, hedge funds and corporate clients to enhance decision-making, optimize portfolios and identify emerging opportunities in global markets. Our commitment to accuracy, integrity and innovation sets us apart. We specialise in delivering actionable optimisation solutions for portfolio and trade structures, supporting asset managers across four continents.

Delta Strategies was founded by Sadat Mannan with over 25 years of experience in global financial industry, including 19 years at Barclays Investment Bank in London and New York.

Our Mission

To enable Treasurers and CIOs to optimise their Prime Broker, OTC Derivative and Exchange relationships; leading to significant enhancements in returns, enhancing yield on cash balances, reducing financing costs, improved Sharpe ratios and reduced costs through cross-asset portfolio optimisation and collateral savings.

Our Approach

Incremental Efficiency Gains

We believe in achieving optimisation through incremental efficiency gains, similar to the continuous improvements seen in engineering projects like SpaceX's Raptor engines.
